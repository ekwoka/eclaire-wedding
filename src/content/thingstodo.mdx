## Things to do in Seoul

While Korea often ends up skipped over as a bucket list destination, Seoul is packed with exciting things to do, see, and eat!

### Cafes

Korea has a very well developed cafe culture, with many beautique cafes lining the streets and tucked in back alleys. There's always a new place to discover. They also have some of the wildest Starbucks you will find if you're less adventurous.
