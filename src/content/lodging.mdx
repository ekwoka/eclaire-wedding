### Youthful Nightlife: Hongdae/Shinchon

This is the area where we met and where the groom lived for 3 years! Hongdae is a popular area for young people with cafes, bars, and clubs. It is nestled near 5 universities (Hongdae is short for 'Hongik Daehakyo' or Hongik University), so there is plenty of affordable eating and fun. We spent a lot of time here!

The area can be separated into a few sections:

- Nightlife and Shopping: Hongdae
- Beautique cafes and bars: Yeonnam-dong
- Cheap good eats and cozy working cafes: Sinchon
